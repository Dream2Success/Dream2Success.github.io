Work At Development Seed!

Join a dynamic team of driven designers, developers, and strategists

  • Product Design Lead

We are a lean, hungry team that builds impactful software and draws powerful insights from complex data. We are a passionate group of artists, rocket scientists, teachers, and public health researchers with some crazy stories to tell. Nearly everyone at Development Seed is a builder with some blend of design and engineering skills. We process, analyze, and visualize data that is constantly changing and improving, so we build our team with folks who are always willing to adapt and evolve.

We’re interested in the best ideas and the right solutions, and we don’t let ego or the status quo get in the way of pursuing them. We work as a team, so instead of a manager, you’ll have a group of collaborators to help and challenge you to produce the best possible tools aimed at some of the world’s hardest problems. We minimize time spent in meetings, and prefer to communicate through GitHub, Slack, and concise daily scrums.

We are currently hiring for these full time positions:

  • Product Design Lead

If the positions above aren’t a perfect fit, get in touch anyway. We are always looking to talk with people who would be a great fit for the team. Some general qualities that we’re looking for are:

  • Motivated You are determined to leave things better than you found them, and you don’t need to be asked to fix what’s broken.
  • Inquisitive You ask good questions and get to the heart of a problem.
  • Excited You want to use great web design and efficient backends to change the process of research and make complex information accessible to wider audiences and decision-makers.
  • Driven - You’re curious and hungry to learn new subjects and skills.
